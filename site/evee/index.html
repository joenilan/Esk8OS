New in v0.9.4

A face that watches the pack.

Range meters read the battery at rest. The safety face reads it under load — the number that actually decides whether you make it home. It tracks your loaded cell voltage live and spells out the state in words: PACK OK, TURN HOME, VOLT SAG, LIMP HOME.

  • MIN Vlowest pack voltage seen under load this session
  • SAGcount of voltage-sag events — a tired pack sags first
  • LIMPremaining range at limp-home throttle, not cruise
  • LOWseconds spent below the get-home voltage floor

The platform

One platform. Editions per vehicle.

ESK8OS

Available

The electric-skateboard edition. Reads dual VESCs over UART and CAN, drives four HUD faces and eight telemetry pages, learns your real Wh/mi, and warns you before the battery lies to you.

  • LilyGo T-Display-S3 · ESP32-S3 OLED · headless
  • Li-ion SoC curve + sag compensation
  • Session CSV logs · WiFi export · OTA

E-bike / scooter

Planned

The same telemetry core, tuned for bar-mounted displays and bigger packs. The companion protocol already carries a vehicle type — the scan list shows the right icon today.

Companion app

Available

Android app with live telemetry, GPS ride recording, trip history with map playback, graphs, GPX export, and full board settings — over BLE, in your pocket, screen off.
