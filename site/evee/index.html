Open PEV telemetry

Your vehicle already knows everything.
EVEE puts it in front of you.

EVEE is an open telemetry platform for personal electric vehicles — boards, bikes, scooters, anything with an ESC and a battery. On-vehicle dashboards, honest battery math, ride logging, and a phone app that tracks every trip. Built in the open, updated over the air.

The platform

One platform. Editions per vehicle.

ESK8OS

available

The electric-skateboard edition. Reads dual VESCs over UART/CAN, drives a color dashboard, learns your real Wh/mi, and warns you before the battery lies to you. Bridge mode lets VESC Tool configure the ESC wirelessly through the display.

  • LilyGo T-Display-S3 · ESP32-S3 OLED · headless
  • Li-ion SoC curve + sag compensation
  • Session CSV logs · WiFi export · OTA

E-bike / scooter

planned

The same telemetry core, tuned for bar-mounted displays and bigger packs. The companion protocol already carries a vehicle type — the scan list shows the right icon today.

Companion app

available

Android app with live telemetry, GPS ride recording, trip history with map playback, graphs, GPX export, and full board settings — connected over BLE, working in your pocket with the screen off.

Updates

Over-the-air, no cables in the rain.

  1. Check. The app reads this site's release feed and compares it to your board's version.
  2. Fetch. It downloads the signed build for your hardware, checksum verified.
  3. Flash. The board raises its own WiFi, takes the image over HTTP, verifies, and reboots. Your odometer and settings stay put.
